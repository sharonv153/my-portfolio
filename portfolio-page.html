Data Breaches Research

This research is intended to help organizations understand how to prepare for & avoid data breaches in the future. For this research uses recorded data breaches in private & public organizations since 2004 to 2022. You can find the original data in the Wikipedia entry “List of data breaches”.

Objectives

  1. Identify which industries are more vulnerable to cyber attacks.
  2. Determine if the number data breaches have increased over the years.
  3. Uncover which methods of attacks are the most common.

Conclusions

Based on the results I would suggest organizations in to invest in increasing security measures to protect their servers. As seen the leading method for hackers is through direct hack attacks. However, it is important that companies also do routine checks to find weak points in their current security measures. This includes adding cyber security training for their employees and stricter data governance measures.

These suggestions are specially important for governments & organizations within the financial, retail, & healthcare industries.

Companies with online products & services seem to be specially vulnerable, thus it is adviced that they heavily invest resources into protecting their clients & their internal information.
